Ticky Tacky

Description

Ticky Tacky is a simple Tic Tac Toe game I made for Android as a just-for-fun exercise. I used Eclipse for code, GIMP for graphics, and my Nexus S for testing.

Requires Android 2.2+. Grab the APK: TickyTacky.APK
